The Power of Math

Math is all around us, and it helps us understand the world better. Below are a few examples:

1. Powers and Exponents

The expression for "two raised to the power of three" can be written as:

2 3

This equals 8.

2. Pythagorean Theorem

One of the most famous formulas in math is the Pythagorean Theorem:

a 2 + b 2 = c 2

Where a and b are the legs of a right triangle, and c is the hypotenuse.
